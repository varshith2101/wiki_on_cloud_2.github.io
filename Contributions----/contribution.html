Bacillus strains Airtable

This Airtable serves as a streamlined reference for the 100+ bacterial strains we came across in our preliminary research. Each entry includes essential details about the strain name, its capability to produce three key lipopeptides (Iturin, Fengycin, and Surfactin), supporting literature evidence, and the strain’s accession number for easy lookup.

Take a look at the table below to find which strains have demonstrated lipopeptide production and find the relevant literature that supports their use.

A Beginner’s Guide to Biopesticide Formulations

In an era where sustainable agriculture is crucial for the health of both the environment and global food systems, biopesticides are emerging as a vital tool in combating crop diseases and pests. This guide is designed to be an accessible entry point for anyone interested in the formulation of biopesticides. Covering essential knowledge from the basics of formulation types to practical considerations for field application, we aim to empower innovators, researchers, and agricultural professionals with the tools they need to create eco-friendly, effective pest control solutions.

A Handy Guide to Stakeholder Identification

Identifying and engaging the right stakeholders is critical to the success of any project. Stakeholders are the people, organizations, or entities that influence or are impacted by a project’s outcomes. Whether it’s researchers, regulators, end-users, or the broader community, understanding their needs, expectations, and concerns ensures the project's relevance, sustainability, and impact. This guide provides practical steps and strategies to help teams identify, prioritize, and effectively collaborate with key stakeholders, ensuring that the project benefits all involved and is aligned with real-world needs.

Promoter Design for Dummies

Promoter Design for Dummies is a handbook designed to guide students and researchers through the process of designing promoters for prokaryotic organisms from the ground up. It covers various aspects such as the nitty-gritty aspects of transcription, different parts of promotors, and essential facets to be considered in the workflow of their optimization.

Consolidated Coffee Yield Datasets for 15 years

A major challenge restricting advancements in the field of prediction of coffee crop is due to lack of comprehensive datasets. Finding viable datasets was crucial for building our model. For this, we accumulated datasets from Coffee Board of India, WRIS, NASA POWER DAV, NATMO for over 15 years. This in our opinion was the best way to contribute to the scientific community.

Access the datasets here
