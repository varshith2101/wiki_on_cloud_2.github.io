Overview

This page provides a comprehensive list of materials required for conducting various experiments in a BSL-2 lab, explicitly focusing on Bacillus subtilis and other microbial studies. The materials outlined are essential for multiple microbiological and molecular biology techniques. This list is a practical reference to ensure all necessary equipment, reagents, and consumables are available for successful experimental execution.
